# Quick Physical Worksheet

Fill this in before your session (~10 minutes with your banking app open), then paste the whole thing into the chat when the Quick Physical asks for your numbers. Rough numbers and ranges are fine — `~$40k` and `no idea` are both valid answers. Delete any line that doesn't apply.

**Privacy note:** no account numbers, no SSNs — this worksheet never needs them. Totals only.

```
QUICK PHYSICAL WORKSHEET — [year]

HOUSEHOLD
- Adults & ages:
- Dependents & ages:
- State:
- Income: single-earner / dual-earner; pay stable / variable

INCOME (per earner, approximate)
- Earner 1: gross $___/yr, take-home $___/mo
- Earner 2: gross $___/yr, take-home $___/mo

BALANCES BY BUCKET (totals across all accounts)
- Cash (checking + savings): $
- Taxable investments (brokerage): $
- Pre-tax retirement (401k/403b/traditional IRA): $
- Roth (IRA + Roth 401k): $
- HSA: $
- Education (529 etc.): $
- Other (crypto, business, rental equity, ...): $

CONCENTRATION
- Any single stock/fund a big chunk of investments? which, roughly what %?
  (check inside your 401(k) too — employer stock hides there)

HOME & DEBTS
- Rent: $___/mo   OR   Own: value ~$___, mortgage $___ at ___%
- Other debts (each): type, balance, rate

SPENDING & SAVING
- Spending: ~$___/mo or $___/yr  (includes irregulars like travel/repairs? y/n)
- Actually saved each month: ~$___, into: ___
- Employer 401(k) match: getting all of it? y/n/unsure

RETIREMENT
- Target age(s), or "no idea":
- Pension expected? (who, roughly how much):

PROTECTION & ESTATE
- Life insurance per adult: ~$___ (or "employer ~2x salary", or none)
- Disability insurance: y/n/unsure
- Umbrella policy: y/n, amount
- Wills: y/n   Guardianship named (if minor kids): y/n
- Beneficiaries reviewed in last ~3 years: y/n

ON MY MIND
- The money question I actually want answered:
```

**Returning users:** paste last year's `QUICK PHYSICAL SUMMARY` block right after the worksheet — the session will open with what changed.
